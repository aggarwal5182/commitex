About the Company

Built in the NCR.
Built for execution.

Nestled in the National Capital Region, CommitEx Displays Pvt. Ltd. is a dynamic B2B solutions company specialising in high-quality fabrication and seamless on-ground execution for exhibitions, retail environments, visual merchandising and event infrastructure.

With a strong commitment to precision, reliability and craftsmanship, we transform concepts into impactful physical experiences that elevate brand presence. Our integrated approach — spanning design interpretation, material engineering, fabrication and installation — ensures consistency, speed and a superior finish across every project.

We don't just build structures. We deliver environments that reflect excellence, reinforce brand identity and drive business outcomes.

Services

END TO END
EXH — 01

Exhibitions

High-impact stalls designed to reflect your brand, executed seamlessly from fabrication through installation. We run turn-key exhibition services — identifying space, creating stall design, fabrication, installation and on-site management for the full duration of the event.

RTL — 02

Retail

High-quality retail displays and POS solutions that strengthen brand presence and influence purchase decisions. We fabricate retail environments and displays in metal, wood, acrylic and more, with hands-on experience exporting retail fixtures to countries outside India.

EVT — 03

Events & Staging

Stage, light and sound — delivered with precision, reliability and flawless execution. A strong network of hotels and banquets brings great venue, food and beverage deals, backed by an equally strong network of vendors for sound, light and technical equipment at any scale.

Custom Fabrication

Precision sheet metal products, industrial enclosures, structural fabrications and engineered assemblies for OEMs across renewable energy, EV and manufacturing sectors.
